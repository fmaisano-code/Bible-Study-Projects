Month 1: Foundation of Friendship

A Personal Study Companion & Devotional Workbook

Welcome to Your Journey: This workbook is designed not merely to increase your factual knowledge of the Bible, but to draw you into a deep, vibrant, and lifelong friendship with your Creator, Jehovah. As you spend 30 minutes on each module, let your heart be shaped by His profound qualities. Approach each study as a personal, guided walk with Him.
